THE GAME:
A sneak peek

  • DEGY game is a multiplayer DeFi blockchain game.

    Players send their deliverymen on missions and get rewarded with in-game tokens. The more deliverymen you have in your squad, the more missions you can accomplish, and the more is your reward.

  • You can spend it on a variety of objects...

    ...features and other add-ons that will boost your performance and enhance the gameplay. After the game launch, the game token will be listed on exchanges. Players who want to develop faster, will buy tokens from other holders.
